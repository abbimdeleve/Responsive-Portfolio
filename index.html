About Me

Hi, I'm Abbi. I'm a writing, reading, language, creation--anything imaginative--enthusiast. Having a vivid imagination is wonderful, (especially when I overthink things and come up with insane scenarios in my head) but it doesn't always get you paid. Enter web development. My plan is to hone said writing and creative skills with the experience I gain at Georgia Tech to construct a career for myself. My hope is that I can, at least: keep up with this ever-advancing world, and at best: change it for the better.
